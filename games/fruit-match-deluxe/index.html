Fruit Match Deluxe FAQ

How do power-ups work?

Each combo fills your power meter. When full, tap a power-up to clear rows, columns, or entire fruit clusters.

Is there a leaderboard?

Yes. Sign in with your social account to record scores and compete with other Fruit Connect fans.

Can I play offline?

Fruit Match Deluxe requires an internet connection to load, but once started you can keep matching even with spotty Wi-Fi.
