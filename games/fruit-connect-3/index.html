How to play Fruit Connect 3

  • Connect identical fruits with a path that bends no more than twice.
  • Beat the countdown timer to keep your streak alive.
  • Trigger combo gauges to clear extra rows automatically.
  • Earn shuffle baskets, hint lights, and time seeds as rewards.

Each level increases the challenge with new fruit arrangements and obstacles. Keep calm, scan the board, and plan the best links to dominate every round.

Fruit Connect 3 tips & tricks

What are combo gauges?

Clearing several fruit pairs in quick succession fills the combo gauge. When full, it blasts a row of fruits and grants bonus points.

How do I get more time?

Complete objectives to earn time seeds. Activating a seed pauses the timer briefly so you can recover from tricky layouts.

Can I play with sound off?

Yes. Fruit Connect 3 includes quick audio controls so you can mute music and sound effects while keeping the action going.
