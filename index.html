How to Play Fruit Connect 3

Connect identical fruits with swift, strategic moves

Basic Rules

  1. Tap two identical fruit tiles to link them with a path.
  2. Paths can turn up to three times but cannot cross other tiles.
  3. Clear all tiles before the timer runs out to advance.
  4. Use hints and shuffles wisely to rescue tricky boards.

Controls & Tips

  • Click / Tap: Select matching tiles and confirm the connection.
  • Mouse Drag: Highlight the path if you prefer guided motions on desktop.
  • Combo Bonus: Chain matches quickly to fill the bonus meter.
  • Power Fruit: Unlock special fruit tiles that clear whole rows.

About the Game

Learn more about Fruit Connect 3 and the team behind the orchard

Fruit Connect 3 is a vibrant line-connection puzzle game that keeps your brain buzzing with colorful produce. Each board is filled with juicy tiles, and your mission is to clear the orchard by pairing identical fruits with a clear path between them.

As levels advance, the grids become more intricate with frozen crates, locked vines, and special fruit tiles that trigger sweeping effects. Combine quick thinking with sharp observation to maintain your combo meter and outpace the countdown.

Whether you are chasing high scores, unlocking new backgrounds, or relaxing with a fruity soundtrack, Fruit Connect 3 is the perfect mix of calm focus and energetic fun.
